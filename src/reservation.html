Rules

Customers must be 21 years or older and hold a valid driver's license to rent a car.

Customers must provide a valid credit card and a government-issued ID to rent a car.

Customers are responsible for any damage caused to the vehicle during the rental period.

Smoking is strictly prohibited in all Teekay Rent-A-Ride vehicles.

Pets are not allowed in Teekay Rent-A-Ride vehicles unless otherwise stated.

Any additional driver must be present at the time of rental and be added to the rental agreement.

Customers are required to return the vehicle with a full tank of fuel; otherwise, refueling charges will apply.

Customers must adhere to all traffic laws and regulations.

Vehicles must be returned on the specified return date and time; otherwise, additional charges may apply.

Late return of the vehicle without prior notification may result in additional charges.

Modes of Payment Accepted:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards (with Visa or Mastercard logo)
  • Cash (with a deposit and pre-authorization on a credit or debit card or bitcoin)
